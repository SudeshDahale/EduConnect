Why This System?

Colleges today face significant challenges in transparency, efficiency, and accessibility. Manual processes lead to delays, lack of accountability, and limited student involvement. Our system is designed to eliminate these issues by digitizing key administrative functions, ensuring fair elections, automated approvals, and real-time tracking of college activities. With this system, students and faculty gain greater visibility into administrative processes, improving trust and engagement.

Leave Application System

Students can apply for leave digitally, with automated tracking and approval.
