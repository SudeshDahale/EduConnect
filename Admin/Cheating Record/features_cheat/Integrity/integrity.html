Academic Integrity

Academic Integrity Guidelines

Academic integrity is the cornerstone of our educational mission. All students are expected to adhere to the following principles:

  • Honesty: Submit original work and give proper credit to others' ideas.
  • Trust: Foster an environment of trust and fairness.
  • Fairness: Ensure equal opportunities for all students.
  • Respect: Respect the intellectual property of others.
  • Responsibility: Take responsibility for your actions and their consequences.

Violations of academic integrity, such as plagiarism, cheating, or falsification, will result in disciplinary action.
