Resolution Workflow

Workflow Stages

1. Complaint Received

Complaint is submitted and logged into the system.

2. Under Review

Complaint is being reviewed by the assigned admin.

3. Investigation

Further investigation is conducted if necessary.

4. Resolution

Complaint is resolved, and feedback is provided.

5. Closed

Complaint is officially closed.
